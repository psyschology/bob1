Frequently Asked Questions

How does bob work?

+

bob aggregates ride prices from various providers, allowing you to compare and choose the best option for your journey. Simply enter your destination, and bob will show you all available rides and their prices.

Is bob available in my city?

+

bob is currently available in major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Chennai. We're rapidly expanding to other cities as well. Check our app for the most up-to-date availability.

How accurate are the prices shown on bob?

+

bob fetches real-time prices directly from our partner ride-sharing services. While we strive for 100% accuracy, prices may occasionally vary slightly due to real-time fluctuations. The final price is always confirmed before you book your ride.
